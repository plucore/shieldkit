## Detailed description (~1500–2000 chars)

> Google Merchant Center suspensions are the #1 silent killer of Shopify ad campaigns. Most stores get hit within their first weeks of running ads — and according to Swell's 2026 Merchant Compliance Report, **60% of suspensions stem from "misrepresentation"**: missing or inconsistent policies, hidden fees, sketchy product imagery, weak structured data. Once Google flags you, your campaigns stop spending overnight.
>
> Recovery agencies (KeyCommerce, StubGroup, FeedArmy) charge **$500 to $3,000** to fight a suspension you could have prevented for the price of a coffee subscription. ShieldKit is the prevention layer.
>
> **The 12-Point GMC Compliance Audit** runs against your live storefront and surfaces the exact issues Google's automated review flags. Sample checks:
>
> - Refund, shipping, privacy, and terms policy completeness — not just present, but with the disclosures Google requires
> - Product JSON-LD structured data with required fields (name, image, offers, availability)
> - Hidden fee detection — surcharges that show up at checkout but aren't disclosed in policy
> - Dropshipper image hosting audit — flags products with images served from CJDropshipping, AliExpress CDNs, etc.
> - Storefront accessibility, mobile page speed, business identity consistency, contact information visibility
>
> **Shield Pro ($14/month)** adds continuous weekly monitoring with a Monday-morning health digest email, an AI-powered policy generator (drafts compliant T&Cs / refund / shipping / privacy policies sized to your store), and a GMC re-review appeal letter generator for when you've already been suspended.
>
> **Shield Max ($39/month)** adds AI commerce readiness. Full Merchant Listings JSON-LD enrichment, a GTIN / MPN / brand auto-filler that bulk-writes the identifiers Google demands, Organization and WebSite schema applied site-wide, an llms.txt file served at your root domain, and an AI-bot allow/block toggle. Your products show up correctly in Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT shopping results, and Perplexity product cards.
>
> Adobe's Q1 2026 retail report: AI-referred traffic converts **42% better** than non-AI traffic and grew **393% year-over-year**. Shopify merchants who aren't structured for AI discovery are leaving the next wave of organic commerce on the table. ShieldKit makes you discoverable, then keeps you compliant.
